About the Name

We're accustomed to calling Python Universe Builder "PUB", as in a place you go to drink beer. Pubs have played an important part in interactive fiction games (if for no other reason than Douglas Adams' "Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy" which was one of the better Infocom(TM) IF games). It's also what Joe Strout called his original package.

Unfortunately, "pub" is also frequently used to refer to a public download area, so it is often a reserved word (as on Sourceforge). So, PUB is also known as "py-universe", which the SF project name. It's still "PUB", though.

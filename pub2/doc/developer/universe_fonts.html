Fonts (Universe)

Technically, PUB doesn't know or care what fonts you use to access it. Only encodings matter to PUB. But of course, your terminal application (or a graphical front-end, like Universe) will have to know what font to use. Theoretically, unicode fonts ought to be interchangeable, but in practice, they are rarely complete. So you will often find yourself needing to select the font based on the locale.
